# Physics-10060-DTU
Her er en samlet git, så du får den bedst mulige Fysik 2026 eksamen. Baseret på mandagsslides. 

# Fysik eksamens beskrivelse: 
Three components contribute to the final grade. Four lab reports (20%), a 2-hour test in the May exam period (20%), and the final written four-hour exam in December (60%). There is only one final grade.

If you take the re-exam for the winter exam, you have to sign up for 10062 (10060 is the summer test for the current cohort). Results from the summer test and lab reports are automatically transferred. They are not transferred to a later attempt. 

There is only a re-exam for the final exam. It is not possible to redo experiments and the two-hour test, except by taking the whole course all over.
